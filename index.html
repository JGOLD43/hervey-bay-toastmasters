About Toastmasters

Toastmasters International is a nonprofit educational organization that teaches public speaking and leadership skills through a worldwide network of clubs. At Hervey Bay Toastmasters, we provide a supportive and positive learning experience where members are empowered to develop communication and leadership skills, resulting in greater self-confidence and personal growth.

Why Join Us?

Build Confidence

Practice speaking in a supportive environment and overcome your fear of public speaking.

Develop Leadership

Take on club roles and learn valuable leadership skills you can apply in your career.
