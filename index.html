Your first visit is completely free! If you decide to join, membership includes a one-time registration fee and affordable monthly dues that cover all educational materials and access to the Pathways learning experience.
Yes! Most of our members joined specifically because they were afraid of speaking. Toastmasters provides a supportive, judgement-free environment where you progress at your own pace. It's the perfect place to overcome your fear.
Meetings include prepared speeches (5-7 minutes), impromptu speaking practice called "Table Topics" (1-2 minutes), and constructive evaluations. It's structured, fun, and everyone has a role to play. You'll also enjoy tea, coffee, and a light supper!
That's entirely up to you! Most members give a prepared speech every 4-6 weeks, but you can go faster or slower based on your schedule and comfort level. There's also plenty of opportunity for short impromptu speaking practice.
